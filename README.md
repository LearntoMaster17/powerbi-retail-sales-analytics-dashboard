# SG Mart Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

The **SG Mart Dashboard** is a Power BI solution designed to deliver actionable insights for retail business stakeholders. This project demonstrates advanced analytics capabilities, including descriptive, diagnostic, geospatial, segmentation, and customer deep-dive analyses, culminating in business observations and recommendations. The dashboard is structured to support strategic decision-making and operational excellence in a retail environment.

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Descriptive Analytics
- **Lifetime Business Performance**: KPIs for Total Sales, Total Profit, Quantity Sold, and Total Orders.
- **Customer Base by Countries**: Visual breakdown of customer distribution and sales contribution by country.
- **Revenue Distribution by Product Category**: Pie and bar charts for product sales and profitability ranking.
- **Shipping Fulfillment Distribution**: Analysis of shipping modes and their impact on order fulfillment.
- **Trend Analysis**: Quarterly sales and profit trends by region.

### 2. Diagnostic Analytics
- **Annual Growth & Margin Performance**: Year-over-year sales growth and profit margin KPIs.
- **Sales vs. Previous Year Target**: Gauge chart for sales achievement.
- **Cumulative Sales Trend**: Yearly sales progression.
- **Strategic Product Profitability Matrix**: Scatter plot of product sales vs. profit.
- **Returns Analysis**: Return rate by product category.
- **YoY Sales Comparison**: Monthly sales comparison with previous year.

### 3. Geospatial & Segmentation
- **Revenue Analysis by Territory**: Regional and country-level segmentation of sales by consumer, corporate, and home office segments.
- **Geospatial Visualization**: Interactive map of sales distribution across Europe.
- **Profitability vs. Revenue Velocity Trends**: Quarterly analysis of profitable and non-profitable revenue.

### 4. Customer Deep-Dive
- **Customer Details Page**: Drill-down into individual customer metrics (e.g., Kayla Tearle), including total sales, profit, discount, orders, quantity, and returns.
- **Order History**: Detailed table of orders, products, quantities, and returns for each customer.

### 5. Business Observations
- **Key Insights & Recommendations**: Automated summary of business findings, including:
  - Best-performing sub-categories by shipping mode
  - Countries with highest customer base and sales contribution
  - Most used shipping modes
  - Actionable recommendations for business improvement

## Business Impact

- **Strategic Decision-Making**: Identifies high-performing products, regions, and customer segments.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Highlights fulfillment bottlenecks and return rates for process improvement.
- **Customer-Centric Approach**: Enables targeted marketing and retention strategies.
- **Continuous Improvement**: Monitors profit margins and sales growth for sustainable business development.

## Technical Highlights

- Built with **Microsoft Power BI** using advanced DAX, interactive slicers, drill-through, and custom visuals.
- Data model supports multi-dimensional analysis by time, region, product, and customer.
- Visualizations include KPI cards, bar/pie charts, scatter plots, maps, and detailed tables.
- Designed for scalability and integration with additional data sources.

## How to Use

1. **Clone the Repository**:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/yourusername/sg-mart-retail-sales-analytics-dashboard.git
   ```
2. **Open the Power BI File**:
   - Launch Microsoft Power BI Desktop.
   - Open the `.pbix` file located in the project directory.
3. **Explore the Dashboard**:
   - Navigate through the dashboard tabs in the sequence: Descriptive Analytics → Diagnostic Analytics → Geospatial & Segmentation → Customer Deep-Dive → Business Observations.
   - Use filters and drill-downs for deeper analysis.
